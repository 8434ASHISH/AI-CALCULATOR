Suggestions for Tile Selection

  • Consider adding 5-15% extra tiles for cuts, breakages, and future repairs.
  • Choose non-slip tiles for bathrooms and kitchens for safety.
  • Verify tile dimensions with your supplier, as actual sizes may vary slightly.
  • Opt for larger tiles to reduce grout lines and create a modern look.
