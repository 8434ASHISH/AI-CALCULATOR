What is BMR?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic life-sustaining functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production while at rest. Knowing your BMR helps plan diet, weight loss, weight gain, and fitness goals.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is BMR different for men and women?

Yes, due to differences in muscle mass and hormones.

Does activity level affect BMR?

No, activity affects total calorie needs, not BMR.
