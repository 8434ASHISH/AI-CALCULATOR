About the Household Item Calculator

The Household Item Calculator helps you track and calculate the total cost of household items. Manually input item names, prices, and quantities, or use the voice input feature to add items quickly.

This tool sums the cost of all items (price × quantity) to provide the total expense. It’s ideal for budgeting, shopping lists, or managing household expenses efficiently.
