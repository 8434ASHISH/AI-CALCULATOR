What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax applied on the supply of goods and services. In India, GST replaces multiple indirect taxes, while in the USA similar calculations are used for sales tax. A GST Calculator simplifies tax calculation and avoids manual errors.
