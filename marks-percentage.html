About the Marks Percentage Calculator

The Marks Percentage Calculator helps you determine the percentage of marks obtained in exams or assignments. Simply input the obtained marks and the total marks to get the percentage instantly.

This tool uses the formula: (Obtained Marks / Total Marks) × 100. It’s perfect for students, teachers, or anyone needing to calculate academic performance quickly and accurately.
