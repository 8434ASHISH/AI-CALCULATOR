Suggestions for Cement Use

  • Choose high-quality cement (e.g., OPC 43 or PPC) for better strength and durability.
  • Store cement bags in a dry, elevated place to prevent moisture damage.
  • Add 5-10% extra cement to account for wastage during mixing.
  • Consult a civil engineer to confirm the mix ratio for your specific project.
