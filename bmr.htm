About BMR Calculator

Welcome to the AI BMR Calculator, a free online tool designed to help you calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) with precision. BMR represents the number of calories your body needs at rest, making it essential for weight management and fitness planning. This calculator uses advanced AI technology to provide instant results. Available in multiple languages including English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Spanish, Bengali, Arabic, Mandarin, and French, it caters to a diverse global audience. Enter your weight, height, age, and gender, then click "Calculate" to see your BMR.

Why choose our BMR Calculator? It’s user-friendly, fast, and optimized for all devices, making it ideal for individuals, dietitians, and fitness enthusiasts. The tool uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation: BMR = 10 × weight (kg) + 6.25 × height (cm) - 5 × age (years) + 5 (for males) or + (-161) (for females). Our AI ensures accuracy and offers dietary suggestions based on your BMR. This calculator stands out with its multilingual support and modern design, differentiating it from traditional BMR tools.
