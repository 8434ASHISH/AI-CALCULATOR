Suggestions for Concrete Work

  • Use a standard mix ratio (e.g., 1:2:4 for cement:sand:aggregate) for general purposes.
  • Add 5-10% extra concrete to account for spillage or uneven surfaces.
  • Ensure proper curing for at least 7 days to achieve maximum strength.
  • Consult a structural engineer for critical projects like foundations or slabs.
