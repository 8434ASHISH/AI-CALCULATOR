What is Ideal Body Weight?

Ideal Body Weight (IBW) is an estimate of the healthiest weight range based on height and gender. It is widely used by doctors and fitness experts to assess weight-related health risks.

How Ideal Weight is Calculated (Formula)

  • Male: 50 kg + 2.3 kg for each inch over 5 feet
  • Female: 45.5 kg + 2.3 kg for each inch over 5 feet

This calculator uses the Devine Formula, widely accepted in India and the USA for estimating ideal body weight.

Why Knowing Ideal Weight is Important?

  • Helps maintain healthy weight range
  • Supports fitness and nutrition goals
  • Reduces risk of obesity-related diseases
  • Useful for medical and health assessments

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is ideal weight same for everyone?

No, it depends on height, gender, and body frame.

Is this different from BMI?

Yes, BMI uses weight & height, ideal weight gives target range.

Is this calculator accurate?

It provides close estimates using standard medical formulas.
