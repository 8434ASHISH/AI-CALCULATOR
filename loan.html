How Loan EMI Calculation Works

Loan EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is calculated using a standard formula followed by banks and financial institutions in both India and the USA.

EMI Formula:
EMI = P × r × (1+r)n / ((1+r)n − 1)
  • P = Loan Amount
  • r = Monthly Interest Rate
  • n = Loan Tenure in Months
