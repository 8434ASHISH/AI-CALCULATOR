Suggestions for Wall Plastering

  • Use a cement-sand ratio of 1:4 for strong and smooth plaster.
  • Account for 10-15% extra plaster to cover uneven surfaces or wastage.
  • Ensure walls are clean and damp before applying plaster for better adhesion.
  • Hire skilled masons for uniform thickness and a professional finish.
