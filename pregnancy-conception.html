About the Pregnancy Calculator

The Pregnancy Calculator estimates your due date and conception details based on your last menstrual period (LMP) and average cycle length. It uses standard medical assumptions to calculate the expected delivery date (approximately 40 weeks from LMP) and likely conception date.
