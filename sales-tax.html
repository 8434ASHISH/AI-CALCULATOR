About the Sales Tax Calculator

The Sales Tax Calculator helps you determine the sales tax and total cost of a purchase. By entering the purchase amount and the sales tax rate, you can instantly calculate the tax amount and the final price, including tax. This tool is ideal for budgeting, shopping, or financial planning.

The calculator uses a simple percentage-based formula to compute the sales tax by multiplying the purchase amount by the tax rate. The total amount is the sum of the purchase amount and the sales tax. Use this tool to quickly understand the full cost of your purchases.
