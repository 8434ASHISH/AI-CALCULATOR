What is an Auto Loan?

An auto loan is a type of financing used to purchase vehicles such as cars, SUVs, or trucks. The loan is repaid in fixed monthly installments that include principal and interest. Auto loan calculators help compare lenders and plan affordable EMIs.

How Auto Loan EMI is Calculated

Auto loan EMI is calculated using a standard formula that considers loan amount, interest rate, and repayment tenure. Longer tenures reduce EMI but increase total interest payable.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is auto loan different from car loan?

In the USA, auto loan covers all vehicles; in India it is often called car loan.

Can I prepay my auto loan?

Yes, but some lenders may charge a prepayment penalty.

Does longer tenure reduce EMI?

Yes, but it increases the total interest paid.
