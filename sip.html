What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows investors to invest a fixed amount monthly or yearly into mutual funds. It is widely used in India and the USA for long-term wealth creation, retirement planning, and financial goals.

How SIP is Calculated

SIP returns are calculated using the compound interest principle. The formula estimates future value based on monthly investment, expected returns, and investment duration.

Benefits of SIP Investment

  • Disciplined investing habit
  • Power of compounding
  • Lower market risk through rupee cost averaging
  • Suitable for salaried & long-term investors

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is SIP better than lump sum investment?

SIP reduces market timing risk and is ideal for beginners.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes, SIPs are flexible and can be paused or stopped anytime.

Is SIP available in the USA?

Yes, similar systematic investment plans exist in US mutual funds.
