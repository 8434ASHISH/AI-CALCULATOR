What is Salary?

Salary is the fixed regular payment received by an employee for work performed. It can be calculated on a monthly or yearly basis and may include allowances, bonuses, and deductions. A salary calculator helps estimate income clearly without manual calculation.

How Salary is Calculated

Yearly salary is calculated by multiplying monthly salary by 12 and adding bonuses. Net salary is calculated after subtracting deductions such as tax, PF, or insurance.
