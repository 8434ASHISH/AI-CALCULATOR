Compound Interest Formula

A = P × (1 + r/n)^(n×t)

Where:
A = Future value
P = Principal amount
r = Annual interest rate (decimal)
n = Compounding frequency per year
t = Time in years

Smart Investment Strategies for 2024

1. Asset Allocation

Follow the 100-age rule for equity allocation. At 30 years old, invest 70% in equities and 30% in debt instruments.

2. Systematic Approach

Use SIPs for mutual funds to average purchase costs and reduce market timing risk.
