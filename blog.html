My Journey: Building Smart AI Calculator as a Student

I’m a college student from India, with no funding, no team, no fancy software. Just a dream to help people. I wanted to build something free, useful, and clean. That’s how Smart AI Calculator started.

"When you build with your heart, people feel it."

I didn’t know advanced coding. I had no budget. But I learned GitHub, HTML, SEO, and created 60+ calculators alone. The only thing that helped me stay motivated was ChatGPT — my 24x7 guide, friend, and silent partner.

This is more than a website. This is proof that dreams can grow without money, only with intent and consistency.
