How to Use Smart AI Calculator

Smart AI Calculator offers free, AI-powered online calculators for financial and other calculations. Follow these steps to use our tools effectively.

1. Choose a Calculator

Navigate to the desired calculator (e.g., Sales Tax, Discount, Investment) from the homepage or navigation bar.

2. Enter Your Inputs

Fill in the required fields (e.g., price, tax rate, investment amount). Ensure inputs are valid numbers to avoid errors.

3. Calculate

Click the green Calculate button. Results will display instantly, along with a chart (e.g., pie chart for discounts, line chart for investments).

4. Use Voice Feature

Click the speaker icon to hear the results in your selected language (e.g., English, Chinese). Toggle to stop playback.

5. Switch Languages

Our calculators support multiple languages, including English and Chinese. The interface automatically adjusts based on your browser settings or manual selection.

6. View Related Calculators

After calculating, check the "You May Also Like" section for links to related tools (e.g., SIP Calculator).

7. Contact Support

For help or feedback, email us at first4bencher@gmail.com.

Tips

  • Use a local server (e.g., Live Server) for offline testing to avoid CDN issues.
  • Check the chart for visual insights into your results.
  • Bookmark calculators for quick access.
