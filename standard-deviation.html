About the Standard Deviation Calculator

The Standard Deviation Calculator helps you analyze the spread of a data set. By entering a series of numbers (comma-separated), you can calculate the mean, variance, and standard deviation. This tool is ideal for students, researchers, or anyone working with statistical data.

The calculator computes the mean by averaging the numbers, the variance by measuring the average squared deviation from the mean, and the standard deviation as the square root of the variance. Use this tool to understand data variability and make informed statistical decisions.
