About the Amortization Calculator

The Amortization Calculator helps you understand your loan repayment schedule. By entering the loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan term, you can calculate your monthly payment, total payment, and total interest paid over the life of the loan. This tool is ideal for planning mortgages, car loans, or any fixed-rate loan.

The calculator uses the amortization formula to compute the monthly payment, which includes both principal and interest. It assumes a fixed interest rate and equal monthly payments. Use this tool to make informed financial decisions and understand the true cost of borrowing.
