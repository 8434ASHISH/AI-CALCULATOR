About the Menstruation Cycle Calculator

The Menstruation Cycle Calculator helps you track and predict your menstrual cycle. By entering the start date of your last period and the average cycle length, you can calculate the expected date of your next period and check if your cycle is on time or late.

The calculator assumes a consistent cycle length and uses the current date to determine if your period is on schedule. It’s a helpful tool for personal health tracking, planning, or consulting with healthcare professionals. Always consult a doctor for medical advice.
