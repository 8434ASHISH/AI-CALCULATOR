What is ROI?

ROI (Return on Investment) is a financial metric used to evaluate the profitability of an investment. It compares the net profit to the cost of investment and is widely used in business, marketing, and investment analysis in both India and the USA.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a good ROI?

A higher ROI is better; acceptable ROI depends on risk and market.

Can ROI be negative?

Yes, a negative ROI indicates a loss.

Is ROI enough to judge an investment?

No, ROI should be considered with risk and time factors.
