The Labour Cost Calculator helps builders, contractors, and homeowners estimate the total labour cost for construction work such as house building, plastering, flooring, painting, and renovation in India and the USA.

What is Labour Cost?

Labour cost refers to the total wages paid to workers involved in construction work. It depends on the number of workers, daily wage rate, working days, or area of work.
