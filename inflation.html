What is Inflation?

Inflation measures how prices for goods and services rise over time, reducing your money's purchasing power. India's average inflation has been 6-7% over the past decade.

Inflation Calculation Formula

Future Value = Present Value × (1 + Inflation Rate)^Years

Example:
₹1,00,000 at 6% inflation for 5 years:
₹1,00,000 × (1 + 0.06)^5 = ₹1,33,823

How to Beat Inflation in 2024

1. Equity Investments

2. Real Assets
