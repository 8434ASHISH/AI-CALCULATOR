About the Pythagoras Theorem Calculator

The Pythagoras Theorem Calculator helps you solve for the sides of a right-angled triangle using the Pythagorean theorem. The formula is: a² + b² = c², where a and b are the legs, and c is the hypotenuse. Enter any two sides to calculate the third.

This tool also suggests Pythagorean triplets (e.g., 3, 4, 5) if your inputs form or are close to a valid triplet. It’s perfect for students, engineers, or anyone working with geometry. Use this calculator to verify triangle sides or explore Pythagorean relationships.
